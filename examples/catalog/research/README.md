# Research Catalog

[中文](README_CN.md) | English

Agents and skills for organizing collected research materials, mapping evidence to claims, identifying gaps, deduplicating sources, and writing balanced synthesis notes.

## Contents

```text
agents/   4 research agents
skills/   3 public skills
```

## Agent Roles

- `research_material_processor`: inventory, classify, deduplicate, and structure collected research materials before synthesis.
- `research_evidence_mapper`: map claims to sources, sections, excerpts, and confidence levels.
- `research_gap_finder`: identify missing evidence, conflicts, weak assumptions, unsupported claims, and next questions.
- `research_synthesis_writer`: draft briefs, synthesis notes, comparison summaries, and reports from mapped evidence.

## Skills

- `source-dedup`: deduplicate overlapping research materials, AI notes, excerpts, transcripts, and source packs.
- `evidence-table`: build claim/evidence/source tables from provided materials.
- `research-synthesis`: turn mapped evidence into balanced research synthesis with limitations.

## Maintenance Notes

Do not add copyrighted source dumps, private research packs, unpublished customer material, or unsupported claims. Keep references to sources descriptive and short. Synthesis workflows must preserve conflicts, confidence levels, and evidence gaps.
